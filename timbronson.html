Tim or also known as Timernator has always been inclined to the arts, whether it be playing Vocals in well-known troupes throughout Cape Town, Dance, and subject for Visual designers and photographers. He is no stranger to the tattoo community as he started his first job at Wildflower Tattoos at Long Street

Tim Bronson

Tattoo & Graphic Design Artist

"We treat each other as family the ambiance at Ganesh Tattoo is always super welcoming and warm"

He loves the first-timer and really enjoys the whole process of the experience. He likes to tattoo and different styles but he most commonly enjoys styles such as Black Work, Bold Colours, Dotwork, and Simple line drawing Tattoos. Influenced by the purpose of the tattoo as a portable convenient art form, both for the clients and the onlookers and with the abilities he has received and studied over the years, he actually appreciates working with customers to produce novel original ideas that they can acknowledge for years to come.
